Welcome to Speed Reader!

This app helps you practice and improve your reading speed using the RSVP (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation) technique.

üß† What is RSVP?

RSVP displays one word at a time in the center of your screen at a controlled speed. This technique:

  • Eliminates eye movement - Your eyes stay fixed on one spot instead of scanning across lines
  • Reduces subvocalization - Minimizes the internal "voice" that slows down reading
  • Increases focus - Forces attention on individual words without distractions
  • Improves comprehension - Your brain processes words more efficiently at higher speeds
